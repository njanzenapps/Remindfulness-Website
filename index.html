Remindfulness - Apps that help you remember to be mindful throughout your busy day

You have learned so many ways to reconnect with your Source, to bring your attention to the Present Moment... If only you could remember to do them throughout your busy day! The Remindfulness app is designed to run in the background while gently and unobtrusively calling you back to the Truth of who you are, throughout your waking hours. In this way, mindfulness practice is integrated into your life rather than being set aside, if time is devoted at all, to an isolated portion of your day.
