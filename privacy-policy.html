Remindfulness App Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 8, 2026

This privacy policy governs your use of the software application Remindfulness (the "Application") and the website www.remindfulnessapp.com and related sites owned and managed by Naomi Janzen (Freedom Techniques). It explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have. By using the Application or our websites, you consent to the practices described in this policy.

The ApplicationThe Remindfulness Application delivers practice reminders and lets you share reminders and the app with others. The core reminder features work on your device and do not require you to create an account or provide personal information to us.
​
Sharing features: When you share a reminder or the app link, you may use your device's standard share options (for example email, text message, or other apps installed on your device). We do not collect or store the email addresses, phone numbers, or contacts you use when sharing, and this information is not transmitted to us or to any third party by the Application.

AdvertisingThe free version of the Application displays advertising provided by Google, through the Google Mobile Ads SDK (AdMob). To serve ads, Google may collect and process certain information from your device, which can include your device's advertising identifier, IP address, device type and operating system, general location inferred from your IP address, and information about how you interact with ads. This information is used by Google to serve and measure ads.

Depending on your consent and settings, ads may be personalised (based on your interests) or non-personalised. Google's use of data is governed by Google's own privacy policy, available at https://policies.google.com/privacy. You can learn more about how Google uses information from apps that use its services at https://policies.google.com/technologies/partner-sites.

Removing ads: The Application offers a one-time in-app purchase that removes advertising. If you purchase this, ads and the associated ad-related data collection by the ad SDK no longer apply within the Application.

App Tracking Transparency (Apple devices)On Apple devices, before any advertising identifier is used to personalise ads, the Application will ask for your permission through Apple's App Tracking Transparency prompt. You may choose to allow or decline. If you decline, you will still see ads, but they will not be personalised using the advertising identifier. You can change this choice at any time in your device's Settings under Privacy and Security.

Consent for users in the European Economic Area, UK and SwitzerlandIf you are located in the European Economic Area, the United Kingdom, or Switzerland, the Application will present a consent message (provided through Google's User Messaging Platform) before serving personalised ads, in accordance with the EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and the ePrivacy Directive. You may consent to or decline the use of your data for personalised advertising, and you may change your choice later where a privacy options control is provided.

Information collected automaticallyThe Application and our websites may automatically collect limited technical information such as device type, operating system, unique device or advertising identifiers, IP address, and information about how the Application or site is used. On our websites, this may include browser type, pages viewed, and time spent. This information is used to operate and improve the Application and websites, to serve and measure advertising as described above, and to help diagnose technical problems.

Crash and diagnostic informationIf the Application experiences a problem, diagnostic information may be generated on your device. We can only access such a diagnostic file if you choose to send it to us, for example when contacting support about a specific issue. Diagnostic files of this kind do not contain information that identifies you personally.

LocationThe Application does not request precise location (such as GPS). As noted above, advertising services may infer a general, approximate location from your IP address for the purpose of serving ads.

Our mailing list and websiteYou can visit our websites without providing personal information. If you choose to join a mailing list belonging to Naomi Janzen, we may collect your name, email address, and related contact details, as well as information about how you interact with our emails (such as which links you click), to help us send you relevant updates.

Our email and website services (for example our email management provider BirdSend and analytics tools) may collect technical and routing information such as browser type, operating system, and IP address to facilitate and measure your use of the site.

How we use your informationWe use the information we collect to:
  • Operate, maintain, and improve the Application and our websites;
  • Serve and measure advertising in the free version of the Application;
  • Respond to support requests and communicate with you about your enquiries;
  • Send you updates or promotional information where you have opted in, from which you can unsubscribe at any time;
  • Process any purchases you make;
  • Comply with our legal obligations and protect our rights.
We do not sell or rent your personal information, and we do not share it with third parties for their own marketing purposes.

Sharing with service providersWe share personal information only with companies that perform services on our behalf (such as email delivery, payment processing, analytics, and advertising), and only to the extent necessary for them to perform those services. We require these providers to protect your information consistently with this policy. Advertising is provided by Google as described in the Advertising section above.

Data retentionWe retain your information for as long as needed to provide services to you, or as required to meet our legal obligations. If you wish to have your information deleted, contact us at admin@remindfulnessapp.com and we will respond within a reasonable period.

Your choices and rightsYou may opt out of marketing emails at any time using the unsubscribe link in any email we send. We may still send you essential service-related messages. Depending on where you live, you may have rights to access, correct, or delete the personal information we hold about you. To make such a request, contact us at admin[at]remindfulnessapp.com

Individuals in the European Economic Area, the United Kingdom, and certain other regions have specific statutory rights regarding their personal data, including the right to access, correct, or delete it, and to object to processing for direct marketing. Where our processing is subject to the GDPR, we rely on your consent for personalised advertising and on our legitimate interests for other processing described in this policy.

Data storage and international transferYour information may be stored and processed in Australia and in other countries where we or our service providers operate. By using the Application or our websites, you consent to the transfer and processing of your information in these locations.

Cookies and tracking technologies (websites)Our websites may use cookies and similar technologies to operate the site, remember your preferences, measure traffic, and improve our services. Most browsers let you refuse or delete cookies; doing so may affect how the site functions.

Links to other websitesThe Application and our websites may contain links to other sites whose privacy practices differ from ours. We encourage you to read the privacy policy of any site you visit. We are not responsible for the practices of third-party sites.

ChildrenThe Application and our websites are not directed to children under the age of 18, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under 18. If you believe a child under 18 has provided personal information, please contact us at admin[at]remindfulnessapp.com and we will delete it.

Changes to this policyWe may update this privacy policy from time to time. When we make material changes, we will post the updated policy here and, where appropriate, notify you. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.

Contact usIf you have any questions about this privacy policy or our privacy practices, please contact us at admin[at]remindfulnessapp.com.
